The Studio

Atelier Nine is a design-driven practice founded on the belief that architecture is the quiet partner in every meaningful moment. We pursue clarity over decoration, substance over spectacle.

Based in Copenhagen with projects across three continents, our team of eighteen architects and designers brings a collective rigor to every scale — from a door handle to a city block.
