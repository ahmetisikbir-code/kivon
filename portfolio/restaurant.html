Our Philosophy

Shun — The Peak of the Season

Chef Kenji Tanaka spent two decades mastering the art of washoku across Kyoto, Osaka, and Tokyo before opening Sakura in 1998. His approach is rooted in shun — serving each ingredient at the precise moment it reaches its peak.

Every morning, Chef Tanaka visits Toyosu Market himself, selecting fish that was swimming hours before. Vegetables arrive from a single farm in Kanagawa, grown exclusively for Sakura.

“We do not cook to impress. We cook to honor the ingredient.”
