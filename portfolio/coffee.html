Our Story

Rooted in the soil, raised with care.

We travel to origin. We meet the farmers. We taste the cherries right off the branch.

Every bean we roast carries a story — of the land it grew on, the hands that picked it, and the community that nurtured it. We partner directly with smallholder farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Rwanda, paying premiums well above Fair Trade to ensure sustainable livelihoods.

Back home, each batch is roasted on our vintage Probat drum roaster, tuned by hand to unlock the unique character of each origin. No two roasts are exactly alike — and that is precisely the point.
