The Essence of Luxury

A sanctuary of timeless elegance on the Bodrum Peninsula

Azure Palace Resort & Spa rises from the crystalline waters of the Aegean as a beacon of understated opulence. Every detail has been meticulously curated — from our hand-laid Turkish marble corridors to the private infinity pools that dissolve into the horizon. Here, centuries of Anatolian hospitality converge with contemporary refinement to create an experience that transcends the ordinary.

Set across twelve acres of terraced gardens cascading toward a private white-sand beach, our resort offers just 86 suites and villas — each a sanctuary of space, light, and tranquillity.
