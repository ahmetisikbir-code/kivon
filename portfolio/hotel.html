The Essence of Luxury

A sanctuary of timeless elegance

Azure Palace Resort & Spa rises from the crystalline waters of the Aegean as a beacon of understated opulence. Every detail has been meticulously curated — from our hand-laid Turkish marble corridors to the private infinity pools that dissolve into the horizon.

Set across twelve acres of terraced gardens cascading toward a private white-sand beach, our resort offers just 86 suites and villas — each a sanctuary of space, light, and absolute tranquillity.
