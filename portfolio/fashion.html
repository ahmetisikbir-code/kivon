VEIL

The Art of
Understated Luxury

VEIL is founded on the principle that true luxury whispers. Our SS24 collection explores the tension between structure and fluidity — sharp tailoring softened by gossamer silks, architectural silhouettes rendered in the finest natural fibres.

Each piece is crafted in our Italian atelier, where generations of artisanship meet a distinctly modern sensibility. The result is a wardrobe that transcends seasons, designed for those who understand that elegance is not about being seen, but about being remembered.
